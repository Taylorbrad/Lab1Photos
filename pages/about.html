All about me!


My name is Jocilyn, but my friends know me as Jocie! I am a natural light, lifestyle photographer based out of Snowflake Arizona. I love to enjoy life with my husband and our three little kids, Aaron, Ellie & Rosie.

My passions include family, photography, singing, chocolate, making new friends, and spending time with old ones.

I started photographing people in 2012 & haven't stopped. I love to capture emotion and tell a story with images. Life is beautiful and I find joy capturing yours through my camera.

I'd love to meet you and take your photos! If you think we're a good fit, fill out the contact form below & I'll be in touch.
